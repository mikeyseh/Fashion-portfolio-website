Fashion Portfolio

Into the world of Elachim I would like to introduce myself as somewhat someone who is still discovering himself. I am someone who first and foremost passionate about Jesus yet I'm not to uptight about it. I have interest in high-fashion like Balenciaga but I also love streetwear. Another sense of fashion I have is of Rock Music culture. I have no particular experience in fashion as of yet but thats what Im aiming to gain , the only thing i have done is model at an event at a young age. My design philosophy centers on a fusion of high fashion and streetwear, deeply influenced by hip-hop and rock music. Drawing from diverse cultural experiences across suburban, middle-class, lower-class, and village life, my work embodies a distinct love for classical yet swagger-infused style. I aim to create designs that scream authenticity—nonchalant, chaotic, and simple—while reflecting the human experience. By blending luxurious materials with urban aesthetics, I prioritize comfort and freedom of expression. Each piece tells a story, merging effortless cool with timeless elegance, inviting wearers to embrace their individuality and swagger.
